Blog — Remove featured image in blog posts

Adrian
Updated January 14, 2016 20:39

You can choosing the hide the featured image inside the post page if you'd like to remove the cover image. The image will still show up on the blog page where all the posts are listed.

When editing a post, find the "Post Options" box and simply uncheck the option that says "Show Featured Image". This is what you should be looking for on your screen:
