How to disable the product reviews?

Adrian
Updated December 08, 2016 15:23

 

Disabling the product reviews for one product

When editing a product, go for the Advanced tab and uncheck the review options.

 


Disable the reviews on all products as once

There’s plugin for that (free): https://wordpress.org/plugins/disable-woocommerce-reviews/

This plugin will disable WooCommerce product reviews across your entire shop. There are no settings, simply activate the plugin and you're all set. Deactivate the plugin at any time to re-enable WooCommerce reviews.
